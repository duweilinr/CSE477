Monty Python Science

Science in the middle ages was considerably different from that of today. While modern sparrows have little ability to transport heavy loads, the sparrows of that era routinely flew coconuts from the tropics to Britain. Rabbits, a relatively tame and manageable animal today, were vicious killers, able to rip limbs from bodies and tear out throats.

This site collects up some of the important information about that period of our history and analyzes how the physical laws of that time differ from our own.
